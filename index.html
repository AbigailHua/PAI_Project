Evolution of AI & Its Impact

From an Unusual Pursuit of Checkers to the Intentional Transformation of the Workforce

Tracing the provenance of Artificial Intelligence takes us back to IBM’s research labs, yet today its omnipresence shapes countless facets of our lives—especially within the workforce. This project invites you to explore the historical development of AI, from early game-playing systems to modern workforce implication. Through these stories, we open space to question how AI became so tightly linked to productivity in businesses, why its adoption has accelerated across industries, how it is reshaping digital media, and how rapidly these tools continue to evolve.
